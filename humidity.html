Humidity v. Latitude

Humidity is the amount of water vapor in the air. If there is a lot of water vapor in the air, the humidity will be high. The higher the humidity, the wetter it feels outside. For more information on humidity, visit National Geographic. There is no relationshsip between latitude and humditiy.
